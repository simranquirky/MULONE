AIM

Usually when beginners try to learn new tech stacks, they head over to their official website where they feel lost for a week or two. then again when they want to learn something new again they feel lost. Too many features are sometimes hard to be grasped by them. Our app works to bridge this gap. The simple UI which is similar for every technology makes it easy for newbies to move from one tech stack to another easily without feeling lost.
